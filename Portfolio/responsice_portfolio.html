Artificial Intelegence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) ek aisi technology hai jo machines ko insaan ki tarah sochne aur seekhne ki salahiyat deti hai. Ye alag-alag techniques ka istemal karti hai, jaise machine learning, jahan computers data se seekhte hain, aur natural language processing, jahan wo insani zuban samajhte hain. AI ka istemal har jagah ho raha hai, jaise virtual assistants, jo humari madad karte hain, aur self-driving cars, jo khud chalti hain. Lekin, iske sath kuch challenges bhi hain, jaise privacy aur jobs ka khatara. Isliye, humein AI ka istemal samajhdari se karna chahiye taake hum iske faide utha sakein.
